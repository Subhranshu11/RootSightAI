# ReportOps-Copilot

**Goal of the Project:** To develop an AI-powered Operational Intelligence Copilot that accelerates incident resolution by automatically analyzing operational issues, retrieving relevant enterprise knowledge, identifying probable root causes, correlating historical incidents, and recommending actionable resolutions through a dynamic Hybrid RAG framework and continuous learning mechanism.

Reduce Mean Time to Resolution (MTTR) from hours to minutes by transforming fragmented operational knowledge into instant, AI-driven root cause analysis and resolution intelligence.
